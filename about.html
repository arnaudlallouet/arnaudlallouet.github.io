About Me

Independent AI Researcher & Co-Editor-in-Chief of IJAIT

Dr. Arnaud Lallouet is an independent researcher and co-editor-in-chief of the International Journal on Artificial Intelligence Tools (IJAIT), published by World Scientific. With over two decades of distinguished research in artificial intelligence and constraint programming, he has established himself as a leading figure in the field of AI tools and combinatorial optimization.

Throughout his career, Dr. Lallouet has held positions at several prestigious institutions and contributed significantly to both theoretical foundations and practical applications of AI. His work has been instrumental in bridging the gap between constraint programming theory and real-world applications, particularly in areas such as software-defined networking (SDN), scheduling optimization, and parallel algorithm development.

Dr. Lallouet has authored over 40 peer-reviewed publications in top-tier conferences and journals, including papers at IJCAI, CP (Constraint Programming), AAAI, and ICTAI. His research has been consistently cited by the international AI community, reflecting the significant impact of his contributions to the field.

Research Interests

Constraint Programming & Satisfaction Problems

Pioneering work on constraint games, quantified constraint satisfaction problems (QCSPs), and consistency enforcement in weighted constraint satisfaction.

Machine Learning & AI Tools

Development of constrained machine learning frameworks and innovative approaches to combinatorial optimization using deep learning techniques.

Logic Programming

Fundamental contributions to logic programming languages and their applications in constraint solving.

Game Theory & Multi-agent Systems

Extensive research on constraint games and their applications to network optimization and resource allocation.

Deep Reinforcement Learning for SAT

Innovative approaches using deep reinforcement learning heuristics for Boolean satisfiability solving.

Parallel Algorithm Development

Creating parallel algorithms for global constraint filtering and distribution optimization in constraint programming.
