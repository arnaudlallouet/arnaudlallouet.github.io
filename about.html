About Me

AI Researcher & Technology Enthusiast

I am a researcher in artificial intelligence with a focus on constraint programming, combinatorial optimization, and the application of AI techniques to complex problem solving. My work bridges theoretical advances in constraint satisfaction with practical applications in logistics, scheduling, and optimization.

My research encompasses constraint programming, parallel algorithms for constraint satisfaction, deep learning approaches to SAT solving, and the development of new AI techniques for combinatorial optimization. I am particularly interested in explanation generation for constraint systems and the integration of machine learning with traditional constraint solving.

I have published extensively in top-tier conferences including AAAI, CP, ICTAI, and CPAIOR, focusing on advancing both the theoretical foundations and practical applications of constraint programming and AI optimization techniques.
